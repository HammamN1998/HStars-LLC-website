1. General rule: no refunds

All fees are non-refundable, except in the limited situations described in Section 2 below.

By completing a purchase, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Policy.

2. Limited exceptions (when we may refund)

We may, at our sole discretion or as required by law, issue a full or partial refund only in these cases:

2.1 Billing errors and duplicate charges

  • You were charged more than once for the same subscription period;
  • A clear technical billing error occurred (wrong amount charged compared to the plan displayed at checkout);
  • You were charged after you had properly cancelled auto-renewal before the renewal date and we can verify the cancellation timing.

What to provide: account email, charge date, amount, and screenshot or receipt from Stripe/Paddle.

2.2 Unauthorized charges

If you believe a charge was fraudulent and was not authorized by you or your clinic, contact us immediately at hammamjobs@gmail.com and your payment provider. We will investigate with the processor.

2.3 Legal requirement

If the laws of your jurisdiction require a refund in specific circumstances (e.g., mandatory cooling-off period where applicable and not validly waived), we will comply to the extent required. Nothing in this Policy limits rights that cannot be waived by contract under applicable law.

3. What is not refundable

We do not provide refunds for:

  • Change of mind or dissatisfaction with features;
  • Failure to use the Service during a billing period;
  • Partial periods after cancellation (including monthly or annual plans);
  • Automatic renewals you did not cancel before the renewal date;
  • Downgrades mid-cycle (no prorated refunds for unused higher-tier features);
  • Account suspension or termination due to violation of our Terms;
  • Exceeding included storage without purchasing additional storage;
  • Internet outages, local regulations, or issues outside our reasonable control;
  • One-time (non-renewable) purchases after access to the Service has been provisioned for that period;
  • Taxes or fees charged by payment processors or governments (unless the underlying charge is refunded).

4. Subscription cancellations vs. refunds

Cancelling stops future billing. It does not automatically refund the current period.

Plan typeCancellation effectRefund
Monthly / yearly auto-renewAccess typically continues until end of paid period; no further renewals if cancelled in billing portalNo refund for current period
One-time period (non-renewable)Access until prepaid period endsNo refund after access granted

Use the Stripe Customer Billing Portal (available from your ClinicWell account) to cancel renewable subscriptions before the next charge.

If you paid through Paddle, follow the cancellation or refund instructions in your Paddle receipt or contact us for guidance.

5. How to request a refund review

1. Email hammamjobs@gmail.com with subject line: Refund Request – ClinicWell.

2. Include:

  • Account email;
  • Date and amount of charge;
  • Payment processor (Stripe or Paddle) and last four digits of card if available;
  • Reason for request and any supporting documents.

3. We will review and respond within 5–10 business days.

Approval of a refund request is not guaranteed except where Section 2 clearly applies or law requires it.

6. Chargebacks and disputes

If you dispute a charge with your bank or card issuer (chargeback) without contacting us first, we may:

  • Suspend your account pending investigation;
  • Provide evidence of delivery of the Service to the payment processor;
  • Terminate accounts associated with abusive chargeback patterns.

Please email us first at hammamjobs@gmail.com so we can resolve billing errors quickly.

7. Refund processing time

Approved refunds are submitted to Stripe or Paddle for processing. Depending on your bank, funds may take 5–14 business days (or longer internationally) to appear on your statement after approval.

Continued purchases after an update constitute acceptance of the revised Policy.
