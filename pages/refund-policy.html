2. Global refund rule

Unless required by applicable law, all transactions are non-refundable and non-exchangeable.

Refunds may be issued where required by mandatory consumer protection laws, where a statutory withdrawal right applies within the periods below, or where approved under the discretionary review process in Section 3.

Refund requests must be submitted within the applicable period below, measured from the date of the transaction. Submitting a request within the applicable period does not guarantee that a refund will be approved.

Global baseline: Unless a longer period is required by mandatory law in your country, refund requests must be submitted within 14 calendar days of the transaction date.

4. How to request a refund

4.1 Purchases through Paddle

Use one of the following methods:

  • The “View receipt” or “Manage subscription” link in your Paddle transaction confirmation email;
  • The support or refund option on your receipt or billing page; or
  • paddle.net — select “Request refund.”

4.2 Purchases through Stripe

Email hammam.najem@clinicwell.app with subject line: Refund Request – ClinicWell. Include:

  • Your account email;
  • Date and amount of the charge;
  • Last four digits of your payment card, if available; and
  • Reason for your request and any supporting documents.

We will review Stripe refund requests submitted within the applicable period in Section 3. Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis and may be approved in full, approved in part, or declined.

5. Subscription cancellations vs. refunds

Cancelling a subscription stops future billing. It does not automatically refund charges for the current billing period. You may still request a refund within the applicable period in Section 3.

  • Stripe: Cancel renewable subscriptions through the Stripe Customer Billing Portal (linked from your ClinicWell account) before the next renewal date.
  • Paddle: Cancel through the “Manage subscription” link in your Paddle receipt or at paddle.net. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

6. Technical or product defects

If you experience persistent technical issues or a material defect that prevents you from using ClinicWell as described, contact us first at hammam.najem@clinicwell.app so we can try to resolve the issue.

If the issue cannot be resolved and your purchase was processed by Paddle, you may contact Paddle support through paddle.net with details of the issue and any response you received from us. Where there is evidence of a material technical or product defect, a refund may be issued in accordance with applicable consumer protection laws.

7. Chargebacks and payment disputes

We encourage you to contact us or Paddle before disputing a charge with your bank or card issuer. Contacting us first often leads to faster resolution than a chargeback.

If you initiate a chargeback or payment reversal, access to the Service may be temporarily suspended while the matter is reviewed. We may provide evidence of service delivery to the payment processor. This does not affect your lawful rights to dispute a charge under card-scheme or consumer-protection rules.

8. Refund processing time

Where a refund is approved, it will be processed using the same payment method where possible. Approved refunds are typically processed within 14 days of approval. Depending on your bank or card issuer, funds may take additional time to appear on your statement.

9. Mandatory consumer rights

Nothing in this Policy limits or excludes rights that cannot be waived under mandatory consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction. If local law or Paddle’s policies provide you with greater protection, the highest level of applicable rights will apply.

10. Changes to this Policy and contact

Continued purchases after an update constitute acceptance of the revised Policy.
